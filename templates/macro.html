Data Summary

This map shows the total violent crimes in each state for 2019. Darker colors indicate less crime and lighter indicates more. The middle of the country seems to have very high crime rates in addition to California and Texas. Unsurprisingly, most lower population states like Alaska or Idaho had very little crime.

Takeaways

    1. Total violent crimes is generally correlated with population with some exceptions.
    2. Texas and California have exceptional amounts of violent crime
    3. New York has surprisingly little violent crime despite its high population
All data from The CORGIS Dataset Project.
